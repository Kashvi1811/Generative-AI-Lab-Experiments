## 📖 Detailed Experiment Descriptions

### Experiment 1 — Generating Random Samples
Objective: Visualize Gaussian, Uniform, and Exponential samples vs. theoretical PDFs.

### Experiment 2 — Maximum Likelihood Estimation
Objective: Recover parameters numerically and analytically; study convergence.

### Experiment 3 — Neural Network Training
Objective: Train an MLP on MNIST; observe loss and accuracy dynamics.

### Experiment 4 — GAN Training
Objective: Train generator & discriminator; inspect adversarial dynamics.

### Experiment 5 — Normalizing Flows
Objective: Implement RealNVP-style coupling layers for invertible mappings.

### Experiment 6 — Evaluation Metrics
Objective: Compute accuracy, precision, recall, F1, ROC-AUC and confusion matrices.

### Experiment 7 — Validation Monitoring
Objective: Track validation performance to detect overfitting and tune models.

### Experiment 8 — VAE Latent Learning
Objective: Train a VAE and visualize a 2D latent representation.

### Experiment 9 — PixelCNN Autoregression
Objective: Implement masked convolutions and sample pixel-by-pixel.

### Experiment 10 — GAN Loss Comparison
Objective: Compare BCE, WGAN concept, and feature-matching formulations.
